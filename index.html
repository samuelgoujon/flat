How can I get there from Airport?

  • From Charles de Gaulle/Roissy Airport and Orly Airport: Line RER B to Luxembourg > 5 min by walk
  • From Beauvais Airport: Line 1 to Châtelet (direction Château de Vincennes) > take métro line 4 to Odéon station (direction Montrouge) > from Odéon walk 5 min through Boulevard Saint-Germain to Mabillon station

How can I get there from Gare du Nord?

From Gare du Nord (Eurostar & Thalys): Line 4 to Odéon station (direction Montrouge) > from Odéon walk 5 min through Boulevard Saint-Germain to Mabillon station.
